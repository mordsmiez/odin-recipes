Lasagna

Description

This amazing veggie lasagna will have everyone scrambling for seconds! And it is easy to make as well! Just follow this recipe and you will be all set!

Ingredients

  • Lasagna sheets
  • Tomato Sauce
  • Vegetables
  • Grated cheese
  • Bechamel sauce

Steps

  1. Stir fry veggies in pan.
  2. Mix in tomato sauce.
  3. Stack lasagna sheets and veggie sauce in form.
  4. Top with bechamel sauce.
  5. Top with grated cheese.
  6. Bake in oven until cheese is golden.
