Bechamel Sauce

Description

Bechamel sauce is a recipe staple that elevates your lasagna and similar dishes.

Ingredients

  • Margarine
  • Milk
  • Flour

Steps

  1. Melt margarine in sauce pan.
  2. Add flour and stir well.
  3. Add milk and keep stirring until sauce is viscid.

This recipe works great with this lasagna recipe
